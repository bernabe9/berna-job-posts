## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company operates across San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo with backing from prominent venture firms including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Key Responsibilities

The role involves managing the CEO's executive network, preparing leadership for high-stakes meetings, and creating strategic materials. Primary duties include:

- Operationalizing C-level relationships
- Developing presentations and narratives for enterprise audiences
- Leading weekly business reviews
- Coordinating cross-functional execution
- Supporting quarterly planning cycles for product and engineering teams

## Required Qualifications

- 4-8 years in consulting, investment banking, strategy, product operations, or similar analytical roles
- Exceptional written and verbal communication
- Strong organizational abilities
- Business acumen
- Capacity to balance strategic and tactical work simultaneously
- "Low ego, high ownership" with willingness to adapt to evolving priorities

## Bonus Qualifications
