## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in AI-driven search. The company has backing from Greylock, Unusual Ventures, and other prominent investors, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Key Responsibilities

**Content Strategy & Alignment**
- Leverage performance data and audits to prioritize content improvements
- Deliver measurable gains in rankings, AEO citations, and workflow adoption

**Workflow Engineering & Automation**
- Build AirOps workflows for briefs, publishing cycles, and structured outputs
- Design human-in-the-loop processes that enhance content operations

**Execution & Technical Architecture**
- Execute on-page optimization and CMS publishing
- Conduct technical audits and deploy structured data updates
- Lead keyword research and support site architecture improvements

**Reporting & Insights**
- Create dashboards tracking rankings, visibility, and search performance
- Partner across content, brand, product, design, and search teams

- 5+ years in SEO execution, technical SEO, or content operations

- Proficiency with tools: Ahrefs, SEMrush, Moz, Screaming Frog, GA4, Looker
- CMS experience (Webflow, WordPress, HubSpot)
- Workflow automation skills (Zapier, Make, Clay)
- Cross-functional communication abilities
