## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

AirOps seeks a "high-velocity experimenter" to engineer growth through unconventional channels. This position focuses on building scalable word-of-mouth systems, product-led growth initiatives, and AI-native distribution strategies rather than traditional channel management.

## Primary Responsibilities

1. **Word-of-Mouth Engineering** — Design referral and affiliate programs that transform users into advocates through marketplace-style experiences
2. **Product-Led Growth** — Partner with product/design teams to launch lightweight features generating shareable value and qualified leads
3. **Rapid Experimentation** — Execute 1–2 growth experiments weekly, testing and scaling winning initiatives
4. **Campaign Architecture** — Create high-impact campaigns using non-traditional distribution channels
5. **AI-Native Systems** — Build tools and playbooks leveraging AI for personalization and faster experimentation cycles

## Required Qualifications

- 3+ years in growth, product, or hybrid roles at B2B SaaS/startups
- Proven success with non-traditional growth initiatives
- Proficiency in both building experiments and analyzing metrics (CAC, payback period, conversion funnels)
- Hands-on experience with tools like Clay, Smartlead, or AirOps
- Python coding ability for automation and data tasks
- Action-oriented mindset with strong storytelling and stakeholder influence skills

**Bonus:** Product-led growth, marketplace, or affiliate/revenue-share experience
