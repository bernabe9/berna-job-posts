## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company is backed by prominent investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Position Summary

This role leads strategic initiatives to advance content engineering as a profession through research, education, and workflow products. The director will unify three core pillars: "Strengthen and scale our best-in-class research motion," develop educational curriculum, and productize Power Agents—reusable workflows encoding best practices.

## Key Responsibilities

**Research Engine:** Lead a team developing rigorous methodologies for testing across AI search and organic visibility. Serve as public advocate for research findings across conferences and digital channels.

**Education & Enablement:** Build tiered training tracks converting research into curriculum and platform-specific resources for practitioners at all skill levels.

**Core IP & Power Agents:** Own the roadmap for launching high-impact workflow products delivering out-of-the-box value.

**Cross-Functional Leadership:** Establish metrics and operating cadence aligning product, marketing, and customer teams with insights.

## Required Qualifications

- 5–7+ years in organic search with B2B/SaaS growth experience
- Hands-on AI search channel experience
- Proven research leadership designing rigorous, credible insights
- Exceptional communication translating complex concepts for practitioners and executives
- Curriculum or educational framework design experience
- Ability to build programs from conception to scale in fast-moving environments
