# Director of Data

**Company:** AirOps
**Location:** San Francisco, CA / New York City (Hybrid/Remote)
**Department:** Data
**Employment Type:** Full-Time

## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketing teams gain visibility across traditional and AI search. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Overview

The Director of Data will establish and oversee the entire data function, managing engineering, science, and analytics operations while collaborating directly with founders and product leadership.

## Key Responsibilities

**Data Platform:**
- Build end-to-end data pipelines handling batch and streaming ingestion from crawlers, APIs, product analytics, and CRM systems
- Maintain data warehouse with emphasis on performance, cost optimization, and observability
- Implement orchestration, dbt transformations, data contracts, and automated testing

**Data Science & ML:**
- Develop ML systems analyzing AI search behavior and predicting content performance
- Transition models from research to production while optimizing latency and costs

**Analytics:**
- Create metrics powering dashboards, experiments, and customer-facing analytics
- Coordinate with Product and GTM teams on measurement strategies

**Leadership:**
- Build and mentor a data team
- Drive cross-functional initiatives translating business needs into technical solutions

## Requirements

**Required:**
- 8+ years in data roles with 3+ years leading teams
- Expertise in both data engineering and data science
- Advanced SQL and Python proficiency; hands-on experience with dbt, Airflow/Prefect, Snowflake, or BigQuery
- Demonstrated track record building data products with measurable business impact

**Preferred:**
- Marketing, ad tech, or growth experimentation domain knowledge
- LLM applications and AI agents experience
- Customer-facing analytics product background
