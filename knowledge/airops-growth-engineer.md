## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketing teams achieve visibility across traditional and AI search channels. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

This position focuses on "building product-led experiences designed to attract, engage, and convert prospective customers." The engineer will develop product extensions that drive organic interest and deepen user engagement while influencing customer discovery pathways.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement features exposing new audiences to AirOps
- Collaborate with product/engineering teams on integrations and extensions
- Iterate on growth initiatives using data and feedback
- Build internal tools and automation for scaling outreach
- Align growth efforts with company objectives across teams

## Required Qualifications

- 2+ years in software engineering, growth, product, or startups
- Demonstrated ability executing ideas from concept through completion
- Proficiency with growth analytics platforms and tooling
- Ability to thrive in fast-paced startup environments with autonomy
