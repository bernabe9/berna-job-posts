## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company operates across traditional and AI search channels, serving thousands of marketers. It's backed by major VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

This position focuses on owning the product-led growth engine, specifically managing the journey from initial discovery through early user retention. The role bridges product, data, marketing, and sales functions to develop growth strategy and experimentation programs.

## Key Responsibilities

- Own acquisition, onboarding, and activation funnels for mid-market and enterprise content leaders
- Execute product-led growth roadmap balancing quick wins with revenue-tied initiatives
- Design and operate experiments across signup flows, packaging, templates, and lifecycle messaging
- Build shareable features like audit reports and scorecards to drive word-of-mouth growth
- Collaborate with Growth and Marketing on self-serve programs and SEO-friendly experiences
- Work with Engineering and Design for rapid iteration
- Align with Sales and Customer Success on trial-to-paid motions and PQL definitions

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years Product Management experience; 2+ years in growth/PLG for B2B SaaS
- Demonstrated success improving activation and retention for complex products
- Proficiency with A/B testing tools (Optimizely, Posthog, Statsig)
- Strong analytics: SQL, Amplitude, Posthog, or Metabase
- Experience building onboarding systems and lifecycle programs
- SEO and content operations familiarity
- Excellent cross-functional communication skills
- Comfort with prototyping tools and design collaboration environments
- Action-oriented mindset in fast-moving environments

## Guiding Principles

Extreme Ownership, Quality, Curiosity and Play, Make Our Customers Heroes, Respectful Candor
