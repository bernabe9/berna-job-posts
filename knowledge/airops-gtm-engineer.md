## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company operates hubs in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo, backed by prominent investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Role Summary

This is AirOps's first GTM Engineer position. The successful candidate will "own our in-house GTM Engineering strategy and infrastructure," managing the complete outbound motion from prospect identification through campaign execution.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Outbound Infrastructure:** Oversee the tech stack including Clay workflows, Smartlead campaigns, and enrichment tools
- **Signal-Driven Workflows:** Develop Clay tables identifying buying signals and triggering personalized outreach sequences
- **Campaign Execution:** Design and launch outbound campaigns with account-specific personalization
- **Growth Experimentation:** Partner with Growth and Sales teams on testing new segments and messaging strategies
- **Tool Evaluation:** Assess and implement AI-driven solutions to improve performance metrics

## Required Qualifications

- 2+ years in Sales Operations, Revenue Operations, Marketing Operations, Analytics, or similar technical GTM roles
- Demonstrated proficiency building complex Clay workflows at scale
- Hands-on experience with outbound platforms like Smartlead or Instantly
- Technical competency with APIs, webhooks, and data integrations
- Systems thinking and data-driven decision-making approach
- Proven ownership mentality and ability to execute independently
- Current knowledge of AI tools and applications

## Nice-to-Have Qualifications

- Founder or early-stage startup experience
- Familiarity with tools like Leadfeeder, Apollo, or LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Internal tool or custom integration development experience
- Light coding skills (Python, SQL, JavaScript)
