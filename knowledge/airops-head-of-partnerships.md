## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketers maintain visibility in the AI era. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Key Responsibilities

**Building the Partner Network**
- Convert ad-hoc collaborations into repeatable processes across agencies, consultancies, and AI platforms
- Create GTM playbooks driving measurable partner-sourced revenue
- Design partner tiers, incentive structures, and enablement programs

**Distribution Development**
- Productize integrations expanding the AirOps API
- Create agency bundles combining services and software
- Establish co-marketing and co-selling initiatives

**Cross-Functional Leadership**
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering on integration opportunities
- Advocate for partner ecosystem needs in product roadmap discussions
- Source, negotiate, and close initial 10 high-impact partnerships personally
- Build and mentor a lean team once momentum develops

## Qualifications

- Demonstrated success building partnership programs at high-growth SaaS companies (Series A-C preferred)
- Ability to transition seamlessly between strategic planning and hands-on execution
- Understanding of deal structures, incentive models, and collaborative dynamics
- Fluency in SaaS metrics, product integrations, and go-to-market strategies
- Founder mindset combined with storytelling ability
- Interest in establishing personal brand around partnerships discipline
