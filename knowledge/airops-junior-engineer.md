# Junior Engineer

**Company:** AirOps
**Location:** San Francisco, CA or New York City (Remote/Hybrid)
**Department:** Engineering
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## Company Overview

AirOps is the first end-to-end content engineering platform helping marketing teams gain visibility in AI-powered search. The startup has backing from prominent investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo. We're building the future of content creation and optimization in an AI-first world.

## Role Description

We're seeking a Junior Engineer to join our fast-growing engineering team. This is an exceptional opportunity for an early-career developer to work on cutting-edge AI and content technology while learning from experienced engineers. You'll contribute to building scalable features across our full-stack platform, working with modern technologies including Ruby on Rails, React, and AI/ML integrations.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain features across our Ruby on Rails backend and React frontend
- Write clean, well-tested code with guidance from senior engineers
- Participate in code reviews and learn best practices for software development
- Collaborate with product, design, and customer experience teams
- Contribute to bug fixes, performance improvements, and platform enhancements
- Support integrations with third-party APIs and services
- Learn about AI/ML technologies and their application in content engineering
- Participate in team meetings, planning sessions, and retrospectives

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent practical experience)
- 0-2 years of professional software development experience
- Foundational knowledge of web development concepts and technologies
- Basic proficiency in at least one programming language (Ruby, JavaScript, Python, or similar)
- Understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals
- Familiarity with version control systems (Git)
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
- Eagerness to learn and grow in a fast-paced startup environment
- Interest in AI, machine learning, or content technology
