## About the Company

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketers optimize visibility across AI-driven search environments. The organization is backed by prominent venture firms including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices spanning San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Overview

This hands-on leadership position combines technical architecture with individual contribution. The successful candidate will "shape how brands win in AI-driven search environments through advanced machine learning and data science," designing systems that influence thousands of brands adapting to evolving discovery landscapes.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Technical Leadership:** Design and implement end-to-end ML systems encompassing NLP models, search/recommendation algorithms, and LLM applications
- **Search Intelligence:** Build systems analyzing AI search behavior to identify content opportunities and predict performance across AI platforms
- **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Partner with product and engineering teams translating business needs into technical solutions

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years developing production machine learning systems with demonstrated business impact
- Strong expertise in NLP and search/recommendation systems
- Proficiency across classical (XGBoost, random forests) and modern architectures (transformers, graph neural networks)
- Experience moving models from research to production at scale
- ML infrastructure knowledge: model serving, experiment tracking, feature stores, monitoring
- Technical leadership experience influencing architecture decisions and driving cross-functional projects
- Strong communication abilities explaining complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders
