# Lead Data Engineer

**Company:** AirOps
**Location:** San Francisco, CA or New York City (Remote/Hybrid)
**Department:** Data
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**URL:** https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/airops/2aa7b5f0-ca88-4807-93a6-91e0e69e9e6c

## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketing teams achieve visibility in AI search environments. The company operates across San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo, backed by prominent investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Role Summary

The Lead Data Engineer will manage AirOps's data infrastructure, transforming raw web and content data into reliable datasets. This position combines hands-on engineering with leadership responsibilities, requiring the ability to "set technical direction, write production code, and build a small, high-output team."

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and operate batch and streaming data pipelines from crawlers, APIs, and CRM systems
- Define company-wide data models for content, search rankings, and attribution
- Implement workflow orchestration using Airflow or Prefect with dbt transformations
- Establish data quality standards, SLAs, and monitoring practices
- Manage Snowflake/BigQuery and PostgreSQL infrastructure
- Build metrics layers powering dashboards and product features
- Partner with Product and Customer teams on tracking and measurement strategies
- Evaluate and integrate data tools for ingestion and observability
- Hire and mentor team members, establishing engineering standards

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years data engineering experience with 2+ years leading projects
- Expert-level SQL and Python for production pipelines
- Hands-on experience with dbt and workflow orchestration tools
- Strong dimensional and event-driven modeling background
- Snowflake or BigQuery experience plus PostgreSQL proficiency
- Track record building analytics data products
- Cloud infrastructure experience (AWS/GCP) and Terraform knowledge
- Clear communication with action-oriented mindset and quality focus

**Preferred:** SEO, content analytics, or growth experimentation domain knowledge
