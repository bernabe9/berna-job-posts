# Lead Data Scientist

**Company:** AirOps

**Department:** Data

## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in AI-driven search. The company is backed by prominent investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

This hands-on leadership position combines technical architecture with hands-on coding. The successful candidate will build production-grade machine learning systems that help marketing teams optimize content for AI agents and improve search visibility. The role focuses on "the intersection of NLP, search algorithms, and large language models."

## Key Responsibilities

- **Technical Leadership:** Design and deploy end-to-end ML systems including NLP models, search algorithms, recommendation systems, and LLM applications
- **Search Intelligence:** Build systems analyzing AI search behavior and predicting content performance across platforms
- **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Partner with product and engineering teams to translate business needs into technical solutions

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years building production ML systems with demonstrated business impact
- Strong NLP and search/recommendation system experience
- Expertise in classical models (XGBoost, random forests), deep learning architectures (transformers, graph neural networks), and reinforcement learning
- Proven track record moving models from research to production with optimization for latency and cost
- ML infrastructure experience: model serving, experiment tracking, feature stores, monitoring
- Technical leadership skills and excellent communication abilities
