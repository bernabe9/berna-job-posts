## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI search era. The company serves marketing teams seeking to optimize their presence across traditional and AI-powered discovery channels, backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Role Summary

This position involves designing products that enable brands to thrive as AI agents become primary discovery mechanisms. The designer will own substantial portions of the platform experience, from search visibility tools to content editing interfaces, working with rapid prototyping methodologies.

## Key Responsibilities

- Direct end-to-end product design for major platform features through launch and iteration cycles
- Translate AI search visibility challenges into design solutions for content teams
- Create workflows for complex tasks including content gap analysis and multi-channel publishing
- Develop data visualizations that present large datasets without overwhelming users
- Build and maintain design systems serving diverse user personas
- Conduct user research with marketing professionals and SEO specialists
- Rapidly prototype and validate design decisions before engineering investment
- Identify strategic opportunities where design directly impacts customer outcomes
- Mentor junior design team members

## Required Qualifications

- 6+ years product design experience; minimum 3 years on B2B SaaS platforms
- Portfolio demonstrating simplification of complex workflows and data-heavy interfaces
- Experience designing for both technical and non-technical audiences
- Knowledge of content management systems, SEO tools, or martech platforms
- Proficiency with modern prototyping tools and design collaboration environments
- Track record of shipping products with measurable business impact
- Strong communication and stakeholder management abilities
- Comfort navigating ambiguity and establishing structure
