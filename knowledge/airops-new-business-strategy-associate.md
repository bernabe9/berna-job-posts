## About the Company

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The organization is backed by prominent venture firms including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices across San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Overview

This associate position focuses on identifying, launching, and scaling new business opportunities. The role combines strategic planning with operational execution, requiring candidates to manage projects from conception through implementation and results measurement.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify revenue opportunities across industry segments and customer use cases
- Analyze opportunity size, unit economics, and growth potential
- Collaborate cross-functionally to design and launch new programs
- Establish metrics dashboards and track weekly/monthly performance
- Conduct experiments on workflows, pricing models, and customer onboarding
- Bridge technical and non-technical team coordination
- Balance detailed execution with long-term strategic vision
- Model internal product usage to inform customer value extraction

## Required Qualifications

- 3-5 years professional experience in consulting, operations, product strategy, or analytics at technology companies
- Strong analytical capabilities for modeling and data interpretation
- Comfort with detailed workflow and process design
- Proactive entrepreneurial approach with bias toward action
- Ability to influence across departments without formal authority
- Balance of strategic thinking and tactical execution skills
- Adaptability in fast-moving, ambiguous environments

## Bonus Qualifications

- AI interest
- Enthusiasm for marketing innovation
