## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketing teams achieve visibility in AI-powered search. The company is backed by prominent venture firms including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Description

The position seeks someone to "build product experiences that people love" through cross-functional collaboration with design, product, and engineering teams. The role emphasizes rapid prototyping and shipping features at startup velocity.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and launch AI-powered products addressing real user challenges
- Engage directly with customers for validation and feedback iteration
- Partner across teams to move features from concept to production quickly
- Navigate uncertain situations to determine product direction
- Monitor research trends and identify shipping opportunities
- Maintain system performance and reliability

## Required Qualifications

- 3+ years building consumer-loved products with zero-to-one experience
- Demonstrated ability to ship rapidly without compromising quality
- Comfort operating in ambiguous, fast-changing environments
- Strong product instincts combined with customer empathy
- Excellent communication capabilities

## Preferred Qualifications

- AI enthusiasm
- Python experience
- ML/LLM expertise
- Evaluation methodology knowledge
- Direct customer engagement background
