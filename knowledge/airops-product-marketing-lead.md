# Product Marketing Lead

## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in AI-era search. The company, backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, operates across San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

This position requires orchestrating cross-functional efforts between leadership, product, and sales to advance market positioning. The candidate will shape how AirOps communicates its value proposition and supports customer success across mid-market and enterprise segments.

## Key Responsibilities

- Partner across leadership and teams on product marketing strategy and priorities
- Define positioning and messaging for different buyer segments
- Conduct customer research to identify pain points and opportunities
- Develop case studies, videos, collateral, and web content
- Maintain product-sales alignment through feedback loops and enablement assets
- Create playbooks and sales support materials

## Required Qualifications

- 7+ years of B2B SaaS product marketing experience
- Demonstrated success launching products and developing positioning strategies
- Strong partnership history with sales teams
- Excellent writing and communication abilities
- Data-driven approach to strategy iteration
- Proficiency with generative AI tools (preferred)

## Ideal Candidate Profile

Leadership ability in cross-functional settings, customer-centric thinking, comfort with both strategic planning and hands-on execution, enthusiasm for experimentation, and focus on high-impact opportunities.
