## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI era. The company serves thousands of marketers with tools for SEO optimization and AI-powered content creation. Backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Role Summary

The company seeks a "product-minded AI engineer" to develop advanced AI systems, specifically their SEO Strategy Agent and Workflow Builder Copilot.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop LLM-powered features including workflow automation and content strategy tools
- Optimize AI performance through prompt engineering and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
- Build scalable AI infrastructure with low-latency responses and cost efficiency
- Implement AI observability, security monitoring, and compliance safeguards
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product and engineering teams
- Stay current with AI advancements and improve capabilities
- Work with early adopters to refine model performance

## Required Qualifications

- 3+ years in machine learning, AI/LLM integration, or applied NLP
- Proven experience building LLM-powered applications
- Expertise with foundation models (GPT-4o, Claude) and advanced prompting
- Experience with embedding models and vector stores (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector)
- Deep RAG and contextual optimization understanding
- LangChain or similar framework familiarity
- Strong Python programming with AI frameworks
- LLM evaluation, A/B testing, and feedback loop experience
- Understanding of caching, rate limiting, and cost optimization
- Cross-functional collaboration ability
