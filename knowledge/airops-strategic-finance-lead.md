## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketers achieve visibility in AI-powered search. The company operates with hubs in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo, backed by prominent venture firms including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Role Summary

This position offers a strategic seat at the executive table rather than a back-office function. The successful candidate will "architect the financial engine that powers us through hypergrowth," building systems and forecasting models while partnering across GTM, Product, and Operations teams.

## Key Responsibilities

**Strategic & Operational Initiatives:**
- Financial modeling supporting cross-functional decision-making
- Unit economics analysis balancing growth with efficiency
- Enterprise sales operations partnership
- Pricing and packaging strategy analysis
- Revenue cohort analysis (new logos, expansions, renewals)
- Commission and incentive plan design

**Finance Strategy & Operations:**
- Financial modeling ownership (ARR, CAC payback, burn/runway)
- Annual and multi-year planning leadership
- Board reporting and presentation preparation
- Investor relations and fundraising support
- Accounting firm partnership oversight
- Finance automation and systems implementation

## Required Qualifications

- 7+ years in strategic finance, FP&A, corporate development, or business operations at high-growth SaaS companies
- Track record in hypergrowth financial strategy and systems building
- Deep SaaS metrics and unit economics expertise
- Cross-functional leadership and communication skills
- Builder mentality with comfort in ambiguity
- Ability to scale from individual contributor to team leadership

**Strongly Preferred:** Familiarity with modern finance tools (Tabs, Runway, next-gen ERP/AI platforms)
