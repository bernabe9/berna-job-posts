# Technical Account Manager (Enterprise)

**Company:** AirOps
**Location:** San Francisco, CA or New York City (Remote/Hybrid)
**Department:** Solutions
**Employment Type:** Full-Time

## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping marketing teams achieve visibility in AI-powered search environments. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and has offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Key Responsibilities

The role focuses on post-sale customer management, including:

- Serving as "primary technical advisor and strategic partner for enterprise accounts"
- Building AI-powered workflows within AirOps to address customer challenges
- Scaling enterprise delivery processes with hands-on execution
- Cross-functional collaboration with Product, Engineering, and Growth teams
- Identifying expansion opportunities and driving feature adoption
- Monitoring success metrics and presenting insights to stakeholders
- Leading customer enablement and training initiatives

## Required Qualifications

- 2-6 years in Growth, BizOps, Product, Technical Account Management, or similar roles at startups/B2B SaaS
- Enterprise customer support experience in complex environments
- Hands-on experience with LLMs, AI/ML, or automation platforms like Zapier or Make
- Strong prototyping and iteration abilities
- Familiarity with prompt engineering and AI workflow integration
- Startup adaptability with excellent communication skills

## Bonus Qualifications

- Python, JavaScript, or scripting language proficiency
- SEO and Content Marketing experience
