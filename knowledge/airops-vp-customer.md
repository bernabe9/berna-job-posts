## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands achieve visibility in the AI search era. The company is backed by prominent VCs including Greylock and Unusual Ventures, with offices in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo.

## Key Responsibilities

The VP Customer will oversee the complete post-sales lifecycle, including onboarding through expansion. Primary duties encompass:

- Designing end-to-end customer journeys from contract signature to renewal
- Building and leading a cross-functional team spanning CSM, Support, Solutions, and Education
- Establishing standardized implementation playbooks and activation programs
- Managing customer health metrics including NRR, retention, NPS, and expansion ARR
- Creating structured feedback mechanisms connecting customer insights to product development
- Collaborating with Sales on seamless handoffs and expansion strategies
- Leveraging automation and AI tools to reduce manual processes
- Scaling operations across onshore, nearshore, and offshore talent
- Conducting executive business reviews and strategic workshops

## Required Experience

- 8+ years in customer-facing leadership roles (Customer Success, Professional Services, Support)
- Minimum 3 years managing direct reports
- Proven success building high-growth customer organizations at SaaS/AI companies
- Track record owning complete post-sales lifecycles
- Expertise in enterprise software implementation and adoption
- Familiarity with modern AI tooling concepts
- Startup experience preferred, particularly seed-stage to Series B environments

## Desired Qualities

Candidates should demonstrate customer obsession, builder mentality, strong communication across technical and executive audiences, and fascination with AI-powered customer experiences. Willingness to travel 2-3 weeks quarterly is expected.
