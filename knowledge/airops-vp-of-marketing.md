## Company Overview

AirOps is a content engineering platform helping brands maintain visibility in the AI-powered search era. The company operates from hubs in San Francisco, New York, and Montevideo, backed by investors including Greylock and Unusual Ventures.

## Role Summary

This executive position owns the public-facing marketing strategy for an emerging category around AI search. The successful candidate will lead a five-person team, shape industry conversations with CMO and founder audiences, and serve as the company's chief storyteller.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Category Leadership:** Establish AirOps as the defining company in AI search through speaking engagements, executive relationship-building, and thought leadership
- **Storytelling:** Create compelling narratives across keynotes, LinkedIn content, customer roundtables, and product launches
- **Product Marketing:** Partner with Product and Sales teams on launches, positioning refinement, and GTM enablement
- **Brand Development:** Build a brand resonating with enterprise CMOs and startup growth leaders
- **Community Building:** Expand the content engineering movement and marketer community
- **Team Building:** Develop an AI-native marketing function with new adoption and revenue impact standards

## Required Qualifications

- 8+ years B2B marketing or growth leadership experience, preferably in AI, SaaS, or category-defining startups
- Demonstrated public-speaking and industry conversation-shaping abilities
- Track record building high-performing teams in fast-paced environments
- Proven success in category design, demand generation, and product marketing
- Deep AI curiosity and belief in its role as marketing force multiplier
- Exceptional storytelling ability translating complex concepts for executive audiences
- Willingness to travel 2-3 weeks quarterly
